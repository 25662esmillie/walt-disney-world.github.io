Walt Disney World

It all started with a dream. A man by the name of Walt Disney wanted to create a place for families to come together. It was to be the world's first theme park. But when that dream needed more room to expand, Walt set his eyes on Florida.

Opening in 1971, the Magic Kingdom was just the beginning for what is now the largest theme park in the world. Today, it consists of four theme parks, two water parks, an outdoor shopping district, a sports complex, twenty-seven different resort hotels, a camping resort, nine additonal hotels, four golf courses, and several internal modes of transportation.
